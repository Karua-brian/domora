# Domain Strategy

Not every part of Domora creates competitive advantage.

Engineering effort should follow business value.

---

## Core Domain

The unique capability that differentiates Domora.

---

## Supporting Domain

Capabilities required by the business but not differentiators.

---

## Generic Domain

Capabilities every SaaS requires and should be bought or reused where possible.

---

## External Domain

Capabilities provided by third-party services.
