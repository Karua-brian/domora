# What is Domora?

Domora is the Operating System for Property Businesses.

Unlike traditional property management software, Domora is designed as a unified platform where every business operation is connected.

Organizations use Domora to manage:

- Properties
- Units
- Tenants
- Leases
- Invoices
- Payments
- Water Billing
- Reports
- Staff
- AI-powered insights

---

# Product Philosophy

Domora is built around business operations rather than software features.

Instead of asking,

"What screens should we build?"

we ask,

"What business process are we supporting?"

Every feature should represent a real-world business operation.
