# Unit Domain

## Definition

A Unit is the smallest rentable space within a Property.

## Business Rules

- Every Unit belongs to exactly one Property.
- A Property may contain many Units.
- A Unit may temporarily have no active Lease.
- A Unit can have at most one active Lease at a time.
- A Unit keeps its identity even as tenants and leases change.
- Every Unit belongs to exactly one Property.
- Every Unit has a unique identity.
- A Unit may be vacant or occupied.
- A Unit may have many historical Leases.
- The Unit is the primary rentable asset in the business.
