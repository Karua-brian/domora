# The Domora Engineering Oath

We will understand the business before writing software.

We will model reality instead of inventing unnecessary abstractions.

We will optimize for clarity over cleverness.

We will build slowly enough to build correctly.

We will not add features that do not solve real business problems.

We will never sacrifice trust for convenience.

We will design systems that can evolve for decades.

We will use Artificial Intelligence to amplify human decision-making, not replace human responsibility.

We will always ask "Why?" before asking "How?"

Every line of code should exist because a business needs it.

Domora exists to help property businesses operate with confidence.
