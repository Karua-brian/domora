# Domain Responsibilities

## Organization

Responsible for business ownership.

Never calculates rent.

---

## Property

Responsible for physical assets.

Never records payments.

---

## Unit

Responsible for occupancy status.

Never generates invoices.

---

## Lease

Responsible for occupancy agreements.

Generates invoices.

---

## Invoice

Responsible for money expected.

Never stores payment history directly.

---

## Payment

Responsible for recording money received.

Never creates leases.

---

## Intelligence

Responsible for advice.

Never changes business records.
