# Domora Ubiquitous Language

## Purpose

This document defines the official business language used throughout Domora.

Every document, API, database table, source file, AI agent and user interface should use these terms consistently.

If a word is not defined here, it should not become part of the business language until reviewed.

# Organization

## Definition

A legal business responsible for managing one or more properties.

Examples

- Landlord
- Property Company
- Real Estate Firm

Never call it

Company
Client
Business

Inside Domora...

It is always an Organization.

---

# Property

## Definition

A physical location managed by an Organization.

Examples

- Apartment Building
- Commercial Building
- Shopping Centre

Never call it

Apartment
Estate

Property is the parent.

---

# Unit

## Definition

The smallest rentable space inside a property.

Examples

- Bedsitter
- One Bedroom
- Shop
- Office
- Warehouse

Never call it

Room
Apartment

Everything rentable is simply a Unit.

---

# Tenant

## Definition

A customer occupying a Unit under an active Lease.

Notice something.

A person only becomes a Tenant after the lease becomes active.

Before that...

They're a Prospective Tenant.

That distinction matters.

---

# Lease

## Definition

A legal agreement granting temporary occupancy of a Unit.

Notice.

We never say:

Rental Contract

Agreement

Occupation Paper

Inside Domora...

It's always a Lease.

---

# Occupancy

## Definition

The state of a Unit being occupied under an active Lease.

Notice.

The Tenant occupies.

The Unit has Occupancy.

Those are different concepts.

---

# Vacancy

## Definition

The state of a Unit having no active Lease.

---

# Invoice

## Definition

A financial request indicating money expected from a Lease.

Notice.

Expected.

Not received.

---

# Payment

## Definition

Money received towards one or more invoices.

---

# Arrears

## Definition

Outstanding money that remains unpaid after its due date.

Notice.

This isn't just an unpaid invoice.

It's a business concept.

---

# Penalty

## Definition

An additional charge resulting from violation of Lease terms.

---

# Reservation

## Definition

A temporary hold placed on a Unit before a Lease becomes active.

---

# Move-In

## Definition

The business event where occupancy officially begins.

---

# Move-Out

## Definition

The business event where occupancy officially ends.

---

# Audit Log

## Definition

A permanent record describing who performed which business action and when.

Never delete audit logs.

Ever.
