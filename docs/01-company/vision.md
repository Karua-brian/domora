# Vision

## Purpose

Define the future that Domora exists to create.

---

## Vision Statement

Domora exists to become the most intelligent and trusted operating system for running property businesses.

Just as an operating system coordinates everything inside a computer, Domora coordinates every operation inside a property business.

Our goal is not simply to digitize property management.

Our goal is to make property businesses more trustworthy, intelligent, and efficient.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Domora will become the central platform through which property businesses:

- Manage organizations
- Manage properties
- Manage tenants
- Manage leases
- Manage billing
- Manage payments
- Automate operations
- Gain business intelligence through AI

---

## Success

A successful Domora platform allows a property business to answer any operational question accurately and instantly.

Examples include:

- Who occupies this unit?
- Which invoices remain unpaid?
- Which properties generate the most revenue?
- Which leases expire next month?
- Which tenants are consistently late?
