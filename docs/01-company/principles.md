# Principles

These principles guide every technical and business decision made within Domora.

Software does not change state. Business events change state. Software protects the validity of those changes.

---

## 1. Business Before Technology

Technology exists to solve business problems.

We begin with understanding the business before choosing tools.

---

## 2. Truth Over Convenience

Correct business data is more important than developer convenience.

If a feature risks data integrity, it is redesigned.

---

## 3. One Source of Truth

Every important business fact should exist in only one authoritative place.

Duplication creates inconsistency.

---

## 4. History Never Disappears

Business history should be preserved.

Records should be reversible and auditable rather than deleted.

---

## 5. Humans Decide, AI Assists

Artificial Intelligence supports decision-making.

Business responsibility remains with humans.

---

## 6. Documentation Before Implementation

Understanding the problem precedes writing code.

Every major architectural decision should be documented.

---

## 7. Build for the Long Term

Domora is intended to become a platform used for many years.

Short-term shortcuts that create long-term problems should be avoided.

---

## 8. Simplicity Wins

Complexity is introduced only when justified by real business requirements.

Simple systems are easier to understand, maintain, and extend.

---

## 9. Domora is the Source of Business Truth

Every decision made by a property business should be traceable to accurate, consistent, and auditable business data.
