# Mission

## Purpose

Define what Domora does every day to move toward its vision.

---

## Mission Statement

Domora helps property businesses maximize occupancy, protect revenue, and operate with confidence.

Help property businesses operate with complete trust, intelligence, and automation.

---

## How We Achieve This

Domora focuses on three pillars.

### Trust

Business data must always be accurate.

Every payment.

Every invoice.

Every lease.

Every record.

---

### Intelligence

Domora should help businesses make better decisions using data.

Eventually AI becomes a first-class citizen within the platform.

---

### Automation

Repetitive operational work should be automated wherever possible while keeping humans responsible for important business decisions.

---

## Our Customers

Domora serves organizations that manage rental properties.

These include:

- Landlords
- Property Managers
- Property Management Companies
- Real Estate Businesses

Future versions may also serve governments, institutions, and housing organizations.
