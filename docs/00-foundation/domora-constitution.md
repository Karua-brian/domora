# Domora Constitution

## Purpose

This document defines the non-negotiable principles upon which Domora is built.

Technology may change.

Programming languages may change.

Frameworks may change.

These principles should remain stable.

A repository is the home of the product. A solution is the home of the code.

---

# Principle 1

Business before Technology.

We understand the business before designing software.

---

# Principle 2

Trust above Convenience.

The correctness of business records is more important than development speed.

---

# Principle 3

One Source of Truth.

Every business fact should have one authoritative owner.

---

# Principle 4

Business Rules belong in the Domain.

Controllers, databases and user interfaces must never own business rules.

---

# Principle 5

Events describe what happened.

State changes because events occur.

---

# Principle 6

AI assists.

Humans remain accountable.

---

# Principle 7

Every feature must improve one of:

- Trust
- Coordination
- Revenue
- Visibility
- Automation

Otherwise it should not exist.
